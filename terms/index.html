Hi, these are the terms for renting a dumpster from H&L Junk Removal and Hauling Solutions, LLC ("H&L," "we," "us," or "our"). We have kept them short and in plain English, because you should be able to read them in a couple of minutes and know exactly what you are getting. When you book a rental with us, by phone, text, or online, you agree to these terms.

These terms cover our dumpster rentals. Our full-service junk removal is quoted per job, so the price and scope for that work is whatever we agree on in your quote.

Pricing and Rental Periods

We rent one size: a 14-yard roll-off. We offer two rental periods:

  • 3 days for $399
  • 7 days for $475

Both prices include delivery, the rental period, and haul-off. There is no separate delivery fee, no fuel surcharge, and no weight or tonnage fee.

Not sure how long you need? Take the 7-day. It costs less than adding a single extra day to the 3-day rental, so if there is any chance your job runs long, the 7-day is the cheaper choice up front.

Extra Days

If you keep the dumpster past the rental period you booked, extra days are $100 per day. That applies from day 4 on a 3-day rental, and from day 8 on a 7-day rental.

We charge this because we run a small local fleet, and a can sitting on a finished job is a can another neighbor cannot rent. If your job runs long, call or text us before your pickup date and we will work with you.

How We Count the Days

Day 1 is the day we deliver. Your pickup day does not count as a rental day. So a 3-day rental delivered on a Monday is picked up Thursday, and a 7-day rental delivered on a Monday is picked up the following Monday.

We schedule pickup for the day after your rental period ends. If we are running behind and pick up late for a reason that is on us, that is on us, and you are not charged for it.

How Much You Can Put In It

Fill it right up to the top. The only rule is that everything stays level with the top rail, with nothing heaped over the sides. We cannot legally or safely haul a can that is mounded over the top, and if it is overfilled we will have to ask you to take material off before we can move it.

There is no weight limit and no tonnage fee. You are not going to get a surprise bill for a heavy load.

One heads up on heavy material. If you are loading concrete, brick, dirt, rock, or roofing shingles, call or text us before you start. Dense material gets to a safe towing weight long before it reaches the top rail, and we will tell you where to stop filling. This is a safety limit, not a pricing trick.

What Cannot Go In the Dumpster

Texas landfills will not accept certain items, and some of them are banned by state rule. Please keep all of the following out of the can:

  • Hazardous and chemical waste: wet paint, solvents, pesticides, pool chemicals, asbestos, cleaning chemicals
  • Automotive: motor oil, oil filters, fuel, antifreeze, other automotive fluids
  • Tires and batteries: whole tires, lead-acid batteries
  • Appliances with Freon: refrigerators, freezers, air conditioners, dehumidifiers
  • Electronics: TVs, computer monitors
  • Other: propane tanks, medical waste, ammunition, any sealed pressurized container

If any of this ends up in the can, we may not be able to haul it until it is removed, and you would be responsible for any disposal charge the landfill passes on to us for it.

Not sure about something? Just ask before you toss it in. Some of these items we can take away separately through our junk removal service, so ask us about that too.

Where We Put It

We place every dumpster with care and use boards under it to protect your driveway. We are fully insured.

You are responsible for telling us where you want it and for making sure that spot is clear when we arrive. Please keep in mind:

  • We need clear overhead space, so watch for low branches and power lines.
  • Soft ground, fresh sod, sprinkler heads, and septic lines can be damaged by the weight of a loaded can. If you point us at a spot, we will put it there, but we cannot be responsible for damage to surfaces or buried lines you asked us to place it on.
  • Please keep vehicles clear of the spot on delivery day and on pickup day. If we cannot get to the can, we will call you and work it out.

Driveway placement does not need a permit. If you need the dumpster in the street, most cities in our area require a low-cost permit, and that is on the customer to pull. For League City, the Building Department can help you at 281-554-1429.
