Step by Step

Four Simple Steps

From your first call to the empty driveway, here is exactly how it goes.

1. Reserve It

Call or text 832-425-7749. Tell us what you are working on, where the dumpster is going, and when you want it. We lock in a drop-off day, usually same or next day, and give you the flat price up front, $399 for 3 days or $475 for 7. No account, no deposit games.

2. We Deliver It

On your drop-off day we bring the 14-yard roll-off on our trailer and set it exactly where you want it. We put boards down to protect your driveway and line it up so it is easy to load. You do not even need to be home, just tell us where to place it.

3. You Fill It

Load it over your rental at your own pace, no one rushing you. Fill it right up to the top, just keep everything level with the rim so it is safe to haul, nothing heaped over the sides.

4. We Haul It Away

Give us a call when you are done, or when your rental is up, and we come back, pick it up, and take everything to the proper facility. You never touch the dump. Need a few more days? Extra days are $100 each.

Process FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book a dumpster?

Usually same-day or next-day works, especially early in the week. During storm season or a busy stretch, a day or two of notice helps us lock in your spot. Call or text 832-425-7749 and we will find a time.

Where can you place the dumpster?

Most people put it in the driveway, which needs no permit. We can also set it on the street if your city allows it, which may need a low-cost permit we will help you get. We just need a spot with clear access for the truck and trailer.

Do I need to be home for drop-off or pickup?

No. As long as we know exactly where to place it and the spot is clear, you do not need to be there for drop-off or pickup. A quick text with instructions is plenty.

How do I know when to have it picked up?

Whenever you are done, or when your rental is up, just call or text us and we come get it. If you keep it past the rental you booked, extra days are $100 each.

Can I move the dumpster once it is placed?

Please leave it where we set it. Moving a loaded roll-off yourself can damage your driveway and is not safe. If it needs to move, call us and we will handle it.
