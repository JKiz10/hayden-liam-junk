Do you remove refrigerators and freezers that still have Freon in League City?

Yes. Fridges, freezers, and window AC units hold refrigerant, and it has to be handled the right way. We take them off your hands and make sure the Freon side is dealt with responsibly instead of leaking into a landfill. Just call or text 832-425-7749 for a free quote.

Can you haul a washer and dryer out of a second-floor apartment or upstairs laundry room?

That is what we are here for. We do the lifting and carry the unit down the stairs and out to the trailer, so you never have to wrestle it yourself. We are fully insured, so your walls and floors are covered along the way.

How is this different from League City bulk trash pickup through AmeriWaste?

Bulk trash means dragging the appliance to the curb yourself, following the schedule, and hoping it fits the rules. We come inside, do the heavy lifting, and haul it away on your timeline. No hauling it out front and waiting.
