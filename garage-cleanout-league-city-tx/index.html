Questions From League City Customers

Do I need to move everything out first, or does your crew do the lifting in my League City garage?

Leave it where it is. You point at what goes and we do all the lifting and hauling. You do not have to drag a single box to the curb.

Can you clear a two-car garage in a single visit?

In most cases, yes. A packed two-car garage is a normal day for us, and we clear it and sweep up in one trip. If it is a bigger job we will tell you up front. The quote is free.

Do you take old paint, chemicals, or yard equipment during a garage cleanout?

We take the general clutter, shelving, tools, and yard equipment. Household chemicals and paint have special disposal rules, so we will point you to the right drop-off for those. Everything else, we haul.
