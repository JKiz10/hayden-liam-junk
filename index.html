Our Story

Two Friends, One
Big Idea

We started Hayden & Liam Junk Removal because we saw a gap — people needed junk removed, but were getting flaky no-shows, surprise fees, and lackluster service.

Growing up in the same neighborhood, Hayden and Liam turned a side hustle into a full-blown operation built on a simple promise: show up when you say you will, price fairly, and leave the place cleaner than you found it. Today, we serve hundreds of customers across the region and have built a reputation that speaks for itself.
