How much does junk removal cost?

Do you offer free quotes?
Always. Every estimate is 100% free and there is no obligation to book. Send a description or a few photos of your items, or have us take a quick look on-site, and we will give you an upfront quote before any work starts.
What kinds of junk do you remove?
We handle full home and household cleanouts, furniture removal, appliance disposal, construction debris, yard waste, estate cleanouts, and commercial cleanouts. From a single couch to a whole-property cleanout, we do the heavy lifting, loading, and hauling. Tell us what you have at 832-425-7749 for a free quote.
Do you work with property managers, apartment complexes, and realtors?
Yes, commercial work is a core part of what we do. We are fully insured for commercial jobs and every quote is free. Call 832-425-7749 to set up service.
Do you handle full home and estate cleanouts?
Absolutely. We do complete home, household, and estate cleanouts, clearing out furniture, appliances, accumulated belongings, and debris so the property is move-in or sale-ready. How quickly can you come out?
We aim to be fast and flexible and can often schedule pickups quickly depending on the day and your location in our Galveston and Harris County service area. The best way to lock in a time is to call or text 832-425-7749. We will find a slot that works and give you a free quote when we confirm.
Do I need to move the items outside before you arrive?
No, you do not have to lift a thing. Our crew handles all the loading and hauling, whether items are in the garage, upstairs, in the backyard, or inside a unit. Just point us to what is going and we will take care of the rest. Every job starts with a free quote at 832-425-7749.
Is there anything you can't take?
A few things. We cannot take hazardous materials like wet paint, motor oil, gasoline, propane tanks, chemicals, or medical waste, since those need special disposal. Just about everything else is fair game: furniture, appliances, mattresses, general junk, yard and storm debris, and full cleanouts. If you are not sure about an item, send a photo to 832-425-7749 and we will tell you straight, free.
Do I need to be home when you arrive?
Not always. For most outdoor or garage jobs, as long as we can reach the items and we have confirmed the details and the quote with you, you do not have to be there. For inside jobs, someone usually needs to let us in and point out what goes. We will work out the easiest plan when you book. Call or text 832-425-7749 for a free quote.
What do you do with the junk after pickup?
We haul everything away and dispose of it responsibly, recycling or donating usable items where we can rather than sending everything straight to the landfill. You get a cleared space without the hassle of a dump run. Call 832-425-7749 for your free, fully insured pickup.
Do you remove refrigerators, freezers, and air conditioners?
Yes. We remove and haul off refrigerators, freezers, window and central AC units, and other appliances. Units that contain refrigerant need to be handled the right way, and we take care of that as part of the job so you do not have to worry about it. Call or text 832-425-7749 for a free quote on appliance removal.
Do you handle storm and hurricane debris?
Yes. After a storm we help homeowners and businesses across League City and Galveston County clear downed limbs, fence panels, soggy drywall, and other debris, and we haul it all off in one stop. We are fully insured and can mobilize quickly. Save our number, 832-425-7749, before hurricane season so you are ready.
Do you offer same-day junk removal in League City?
