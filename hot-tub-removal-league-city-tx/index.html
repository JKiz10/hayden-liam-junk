FAQ

Questions From League City Customers

Do I need to drain the hot tub before your crew arrives in League City?

You do not have to. If it still holds water we can drain it as part of the job. It helps if it is drained ahead of time, but either way we handle the whole tear-out.

Can you get a hot tub out of a fenced backyard with a narrow gate?

Yes. That is exactly why we dismantle instead of trying to muscle the whole tub out in one piece. We break it down in the yard, carry it out through the gate, and load it onto the dump trailer.

Do you recycle the parts, or does the whole tub go to the landfill?

We pull the metal frame, pumps, and motors for recycling instead of trashing everything. The goal is to keep as much out of the landfill as we can. We are fully insured for the heavy lifting.
