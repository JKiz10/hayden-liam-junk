Worth Knowing

The City Won't Haul Your Remodel

This is the one that catches Texas City homeowners off guard, so it is worth saying plainly. The city does weekly bulky-item and brush pickup, and it is a genuinely good service. But its own rules draw a hard line at your remodel: "Removal of construction and building materials is not considered part of the weekly trash collection and is the responsibility of the property owner/resident."

So the pile of demo you set at the curb after a bathroom tear-out is not going anywhere on trash day. Push it anyway and you are into the city's "Pay Pile" process, billed by the cubic yard. Worth knowing too: your residential garbage here is run by Waste Connections under a city contract, not by a city crew, which is part of why that line is drawn where it is.

That gap is exactly what a roll-off fills. Tear-out from a bathroom, a re-roof, a garage, or a rental turnover is precisely the material the weekly truck is going to drive right past. Ours takes all of it in one drop, at one flat price, and you never sort it into piles.
