Before You Load It

What Can't Go In the Dumpster

A handful of things are banned from Texas landfills by law or just are not safe to haul. Not sure about something? Text a photo to 832-425-7749 and we will tell you straight.

Hazardous & Chemical

Wet paint, stains and solvents, pesticides and weed killer, pool chemicals, and cleaners like bleach or ammonia.

Automotive

Motor oil, gas, diesel and antifreeze, oil filters, and propane or compressed-gas tanks.

Tires & Batteries

Whole tires, car (lead-acid) batteries, and lithium or rechargeable batteries. All are banned from Texas landfills and need a recycler.

Freon Appliances

Fridges, freezers, and AC units still holding Freon. We can still haul these for you separately with our junk removal, just ask.

Electronics

TVs, computer monitors, and other e-waste. Texas treats these as recyclables, not landfill trash.

Medical & Other

Medical or biohazard waste and sharps, asbestos, creosote railroad ties, and loose food waste.

Heavy material like concrete, dirt, brick, or roofing is fine, it is just heavy, so give us a heads up first and we will help you load it safely. If the landfill takes it, we can take it. When in doubt, just ask.
